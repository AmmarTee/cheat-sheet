# Code of Conduct

Professional conduct is expected. This is a technical space for infrastructure admins. Off-topic or unprofessional behavior will result in a block.

## Standards

- Stay on topic (infrastructure and sys-admin tooling)
- Technical disagreements are fine, personal attacks are not
- No spam or self-promotion
- Be direct and concise

## Enforcement

Violations will result in warnings or blocks as appropriate.

## Reporting

Open an issue or contact maintainers directly for serious violations.
